## [1.0.0] - 2026-03-05

### Added

- Initial release of Kevin skill
- **SKILL.md** — Core skill file with personality, workflow router, 18-point contract review checklist, EU AI Act quick reference, threat model summary, model contract clauses, and risk scoring framework
- **references/knowledge-base.md** — Comprehensive legal knowledge base covering:
  - AI regulatory landscape (EU AI Act, CAIA, GDPR, CCPA, DIFC, US state patchwork)
  - Contract and vendor management playbook (ACC Eight Principles)
  - Data processing and privacy framework
  - Generative AI threat model (18 threat categories)
  - Internal AI governance policy framework
  - Intellectual property and ownership guidance
  - AI insurance and liability overview
  - Compliance checklists (pre-launch, quarterly, enterprise sales readiness)
- **references/workflows.md** — Ten structured legal workflows:
  1. AI Vendor Contract Review & Redlining
  2. AI Risk Classification & EU AI Act Compliance Assessment
  3. Data Processing Addendum (DPA) Drafting for AI Systems
  4. Internal AI Acceptable Use Policy Creation
  5. AI Product Privacy Notice Generation
  6. AI Threat Model Assessment
  7. Enterprise Customer AI Due Diligence Response
  8. AI Intellectual Property Audit
  9. AI Incident Response & Breach Notification
  10. AI Regulatory Change Impact Analysis
- **kevin.skill** — Packaged skill file for one-command installation
- **README.md** — Documentation with installation, usage, examples, and customization
- **CONTRIBUTING.md** — Contribution guidelines and style guide
- **LICENSE** — MIT License
